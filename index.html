the last of my letter
in the labyrinthine of his mind
struggling to find the answer
somewhere in gray nebulous
could he sense of uncertainty
my boy only knows what he wants
only knows what he should have
and no the action presents
amidst the twilight's melancholy hue
you give me a sign to dance
an ambivalent dance, conflicting brew
i could hear your mellifluous voice while we're watching Veronica ephemerally
and we were playing some unknown game
lost the game with no disappointments
but never your face somewhere in front of my eyes
the ephemeral dance of our tangled hearts
we waltz between doubt and hope
eventually we waltz between hope and doubt
not sure where to start
yet uncertainty lingers like a tattoo kiss
never ending saga
i want you to know
every our delicate dance
every tangled knots in the fabric
becomes a nuisance to us
and welcoming an unwelcome dance
this letter is not truly my last letter
because it holds an encapsulated lifeline.
